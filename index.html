Nostr District

A multiplayer pixel art social world built on the Nostr protocol.

  • Walk around a live online district and chat with real people in real time
  • Customize your pixel avatar and decorate your own room
  • Connect using your Nostr key or explore as a guest — no sign-up required
  • Visit the Relay, The Feed, the Alley, the Woods, and player-owned rooms
  • Tip sats with Lightning zaps, play rock-paper-scissors, share GIFs and emotes

Nostr District is a free, open, decentralized social experience. No algorithm, no ads, no platform lock-in — just people on Nostr.
